iPhone 11 – Technical Specifications

Display

  • 6.1-inch Liquid Retina HD display
  • 1792×828 resolution
  • True Tone and Haptic Touch

Camera

  • Dual 12MP Ultra Wide & Wide cameras
  • Night mode and Portrait mode
  • 4K video recording at 24 fps, 30 fps, or 60 fps

  • A13 Bionic chip
  • 3rd-generation Neural Engine

Battery & Power

  • Up to 17 hours video playback
  • Fast-charge capable
  • Wireless charging supported

Dimensions

  • 150.9 mm x 75.7 mm x 8.3 mm
  • Weight: 194 g

Connectivity

  • Gigabit-class LTE
  • Wi-Fi 6
  • Dual SIM (nano-SIM + eSIM)
